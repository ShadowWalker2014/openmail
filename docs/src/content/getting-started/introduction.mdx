# Introduction

**OpenMail** is a free, open-source alternative to Customer.io. It gives your team a full customer lifecycle email platform вҖ” broadcasts, automation campaigns, contact segments, and analytics вҖ” without per-seat pricing or vendor lock-in.

Every feature is exposed through a complete REST API and a native [Model Context Protocol (MCP)](https://modelcontextprotocol.io) server, so AI agents like Claude and Cursor can autonomously create and run email campaigns.

## Why OpenMail?

Customer.io costs **$1,000+/month** for growing teams, has limited API access that blocks automation, and has no support for AI agents. OpenMail solves all three.

## Core Concepts

**Workspaces** are the top-level unit of organization. Each workspace has its own contacts, campaigns, broadcasts, and settings. Users can belong to multiple workspaces with different roles (owner, admin, member).

**Contacts** are people you want to email. Each contact has an email address and flexible key-value attributes (e.g. `plan: "pro"`, `company: "Acme"`) that drive personalization and segmentation.

**Segments** are dynamic groups of contacts, filtered by rules. Example: contacts where `attributes.plan = "pro"` and `createdAt > 30 days ago`. When a contact's attributes change, they automatically enter or leave segments.

**Broadcasts** are one-off email blasts sent to one or more segments. Use them for newsletters, product announcements, or one-time campaigns.

**Campaigns** are automated email sequences triggered by events or segment membership changes. Example: send a welcome email immediately when `user_signed_up` fires, then a tips email 3 days later.

**Events** track customer activity in your app. When you call `POST /api/v1/events/track`, it fires any active campaign that's listening for that event name.
